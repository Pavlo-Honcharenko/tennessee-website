ABOUT WASTE SOLUTIONS OF TENNESSEE

For well over a decade, Waste Solutions of Tennessee has been a leader in debris removal services in middle Tennessee. We are a small company and family owned and operated business looking to carve a niche in our community. We enjoy supporting our customers, our community, and various causes that we see making our world a better place. We provide roll off, demolition, and recycling services for the collection, processing, and disposal or repurposing of various types of waste.

Our roll off containers vary in size from 10 to 40 cubic yards (see dimensions) to service businesses, contractors, and homeowners in waste removal. Our trucks drive the dumpsters to your job site, place them, and when you are ready, come back and pick up the full container for one flat rate.
